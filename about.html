About Me


Hello!

My name is Charles Hager and I'm a junior studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley, with a minor in Mechanical Engineering. I was born in Paris, France and grew up in Los Angeles, California before attending high school in Rye, New York.

I fell in love with computer science after taking my first course in college in sophomore year. Since then it's been a crazy ride trying to learn as much as I can. I'm also passionate about cooking, I teach a baking class on the Berkeley campus to introduce other students to making anything from cookies to cream puffs. I'm also very active in the dance community, being on various dance teams since freshman year. I'm bilingual, growing up in a French and American household with both languages spoken regularly. My dreams for the future are to travel through the entire world and experience the nature and cuisine from every corner of the globe.
